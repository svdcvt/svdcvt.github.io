Sofya (Sofi) Dymchenko

Hi, salut, привет! I am a Ph.D. student at University Grenoble Alpes and INRIA, I work with Bruno Raffin in Datamove team.

My research is focused on improving the data-efficiency of a deep neural network training on a data which comes from physics simulators and PDEs (usually reffered as surrogates). I am a member of Melissa project which develops a framework for large scale deep surrogate training on HPC systems.
